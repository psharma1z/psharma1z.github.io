After looking that the most of the suicides are in the males we will try to find out another dimension where the work can be done in a prticular country or world to reduce the number of suicides.For this now pick the age groups. There are overall 6 age groups 15-24 years,25-34 years,35-54 years,5-14 years,55-74 years,75+ years. We can see that the suicides are most common in 35-54 years age group in the world.The least number of suicides happen in the 5-14 age groups and that is understandable as the kids are less suseptible to mental worries.

We should also be able to see the charts for the yearly trends in developed countries and how they are doing in terms of the number of suicides based on the age by applying filters.The countries like Russia are improving while the trend is not good for the country like USA. The 75+ age group when filtered on the country shows a steep rise in suicides.It may be because of the loneliness which might be tackled by more humane approach and empathy.

Based on the above we can conclude that the suicides are a common cause of concern for the world and a lot of work needs to be done. We will be successful in our effort if we can find causes and narrow down the population which is more prone to suicides.We can see that in developed countries this problem is more common between males. The age group where most of suicides happen are 35-54 years but 75 + are also doing suicides which are increasing. The help needs to be provided to these groups in order to make the world suicide free.
